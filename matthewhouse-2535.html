Matthew House Toronto > 25:35 Campaign

Details

Matthew House is a church located in Toronto whose mission is “to inspire hope, restore dignity, and empower independence for refugee claimants.” It was a pleasure for me to have had the opportunity to design the brochure for 25:35 challenge as part of their fund raising campaign. 

Objective

Working remotely from Vancouver, I worked with the team in Toronto for this campaign. The team had given me a few images and the content they would like to include and I had taken the creative direction of this campaign. As the images weren't professionally taken, I made the images black and white to make their branding colour stand out. This will also allow the images to act as a supporting element to the content, rather than the other way around. 

Role
