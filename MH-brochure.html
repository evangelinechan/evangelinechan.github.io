Matthew House Brochure Campaign

Details
Matthew House is a church located in Toronto whose mission is “to inspire hope, restore dignity, and empower independence for refugee claimants.” It was a pleasure for me to have had the opportunity to design the brochure for 25:35 challenge as part of their fund raising campaign. While designing for the brochure, I liaised with the team in Toronto for content and approval, as well as, the print company for print production.
