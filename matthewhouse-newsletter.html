Matthew House Toronto > Quarterly Newsletter

Details

Matthew House is a church located in Toronto whose mission is “to inspire hope, restore dignity, and empower independence for refugee claimants.” I have been working with Matthew House since 2013 supporting their quarterly printed newsletter with design and print production. 

Objective

Every quarter, Matthew House will do a newsletter to update its church members on what was happening at Matthew House and highlight upcoming events. It was a pleasure to design a branded newsletter template for them. 

Role
